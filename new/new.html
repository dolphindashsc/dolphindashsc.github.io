About DDSC

The Dolphin Dash Student Crew is a club at Ocean Lakes High School focused on community service. Each year, we organize events and work with local sponsors to raise funds for a selected charity. Throughout the year, we hold various fundraising activities, including our main event, the Dolphin Dash 5k race. This race brings together students, families, and faculty while raising money for our charity. At the end of the school year, all proceeds from our events are donated to the charity, helping those in need.
