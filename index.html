Dolphin Dash Student Crew
Dolphin Dash Student Crew (DDSC) is a student-run organization of Ocean Lakes HS students who plan, organize, and host events to raise money for a local charity each year
Events
5K Race
May 17, 2025

Every year, our main event is the 5K race around our school. This event usually takes place a few days after all final tests have been completed, and is our biggest event of all.

Winter Carnival
Janurary 17, 2025
Our annual winter carnival marks the halfway point of each Dolphin Dash year. This lively event, which takes place in the cafeteria, includes many fun games, as well as lots of music, food, and bingo too!

Sign up will be available starting early November.
The $5 entrance cost can be paid at the cafeteria foyer.
