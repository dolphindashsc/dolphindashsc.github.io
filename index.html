Dolphin Dash Student Crew (DDSC) is a student-run organization of Ocean Lakes HS students who plan, organize, and host events to raise money for a local charity each year
Upcoming Events
Main Events
5K Race
Saturday, May 17, 2025

Our 20th annual 5k race! Taking place after a year of hard work and fundraising, the 5k is our main event. There will be snacks, music, and so much more to add on to the race.

Sign up or learn more with the sign up button at the top of the screen.

Registration is $15 on the website and $20 in person.

Winter Carnival
Friday, January 17, 2025
Our annual Winter Carnival marks the halfway point of each Dolphin Dash year. This lively event, which takes place in the cafeteria, includes many fun games, as well as lots of music, food, and bingo too!

Sign up will be available starting early November.
The $5 entrance cost can be paid at the cafeteria foyer.
Mini Dash
Saturday, May 17, 2025

Starting this year, we are also launching our Mini-Dash event for elementary schoolers! Taking place after the race, it will be a fun opportunity for younger kids to exercise and connect with a charity!

Talent Show
Saturday, Feburary 15, 2025

In collaboration with Ocean Lakes Theater Company, the Dolphin Dash Student Crew is hosting a talent show for Ocean Lakes High School students! Come show off your talents at a fun event highlighting the diverse abilities of our student body.

Auditions Sign up: November 6th - December 16th

Auditions: January 6th and January 7th

Tech Rehearsals: January 9th and 10th
